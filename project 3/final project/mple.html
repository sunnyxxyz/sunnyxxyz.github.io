Complex VS Simplex

Life itself is extremely simple, but we always artificially complicate the simple things. But in the anyway, it would be attributed to simple. Some people do this takes a very short time, but some people have spent all life for this. This is not who is to blame, but ultimately it is bound to be a mistake.
